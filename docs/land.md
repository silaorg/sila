# Land

A land is whe your AI [agents](agents.md) live. You can decide where that land is stored - it could be your laptop or a server. Technically, a land is just a folder on a computer with a bunch of files and programs. Unlike physical lands - it's trivial to move Sila's lands around.

A land can also extend its [agents](agents.md) with land-specific [skills](skills.md) and [tools](tools.md).
